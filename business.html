    • Faculty of Busines & Economics

    Faculty of Business and Economics is the pioneer faculty launched in November
    2011/12 at Woreta. The faculty started its operation in Accounting and Finance
    and Economics Undergraduate Degree programs in both regular and extension followed
    by Management and Tourism & Hotel Management as the third & fourth undergraduate
    degree programs launched in 2012/13 & 2015/16 respectively at the root place of history
    of Tewodros II the first industrial center of Africa; Debretabor, Gafat. In 2015/16 the
    faculty also opened a post graduate program in Development Economics.

    In line with the main objective of the university, the Faculty staff members are committed to conduct
    community based research & serve the community on areas of Business and Economics in addition to the
    teaching learning activity. For instance; besides its normal task, the Faculty had offered short term
    awareness creation training for the Zonal Category A and B tax payers about tax proclamations, tax
    assessments, internal control and financial management for Small and Micro Enterprises with tangible
    output. The faculty also train & supports small business owners about entrepreneurship, time management,
    customer handling & leadership & change management.

    Besides the community service, the faculty have had identified numerous thematic researchable Business and
    Economics areas and more than 10 proposals were submitted to the faculty since 2014/2015.
